The orphan relation is used to provide a satisfactory treatment of certain instances of ellipsis.


~~~ sdparse
Ali kitap , Ahmet gazete okudu . \n Ahmet read newspapers, Ali books.
nsubj(okudu, Ahmet)
dobj(okudu, gazete)
conj(okudu, Ali)
orphan(Ali, kitap)
~~~
