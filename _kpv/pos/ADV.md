Adverbs are words that typically modify [adjectives](ADJ), [verbs](VERB) or other adverbs for such categories as time, place,
direction or manner.

#### Examples

* [kpv] _бура_ "well"
* [kpv] _дзик_ "completely; really"
* [kpv] _уна_ "much; many; a lot"
