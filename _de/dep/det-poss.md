---
udver: '2'
layout: relation
title: 'det:poss'
shortdef: 'possessive determiner'
---

The relation `det:poss` is used to mark possessive determiners.

~~~ sdparse
in meinem Haus \n in my house
det:poss(Haus, meinem)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
Sie macht ihre eigene Sache . \n She does her own thing .
det:poss(Sache, ihre)
~~~
